OUR RESEARCH DOMAIN

Welcome to our website, where we explore the dynamic world of product delivery in the context of today's ever-evolving business landscape. In the modern era, product delivery has transformed from a mere logistical task into a pivotal touchpoint that significantly influences customer satisfaction, brand loyalty, and overall business success. This shift is driven by the integration of cutting-edge technologies that are reshaping how businesses interact with their customers and streamline their operations. Our research paper takes a deep dive into a range of innovative technological solutions, each designed to address crucial aspects of the delivery process. Through a comprehensive exploration of these advancements, our study aims to contribute to the expanding knowledge base related to optimizing delivery processes and enhancing the overall customer experience.

Implement a multi-supported chatbot that can handle both English and Sinhala languages.

The problem is to develop an effective bilingual chatbot capable of providing customer support and engagement in both English and Sinhala languages for a delivery company.

The solution is to design and implement a bilingual chatbot system that can handle natural language understanding and generation in both English and Sinhala. This solution exploring language processing techniques, collecting and preprocessing relevant data, and training the chatbot using machine learning algorithms.

  • To understand the linguistic and cultural nuances of both English and Sinhala languages and how they affect effective customer engagement and support in the context of a delivery company.
  • To identify and assess existing language processing techniques and resources for both English and Sinhala, with a focus on selecting suitable approaches and tools for developing a bilingual chatbot.
  • To design and develop a chatbot architecture that is capable of natural language understanding and generation in both English and Sinhala, ensuring that it can effectively process and respond to customer inquiries in both languages.

Recognized the employee performance and identify the improvements

Inefficient scheduling, under-staffing, over-staffing, and inaccurate performance metrics are common issues that arise without a workforce optimization system, potentially affecting business efficiency and customer satisfaction.

According to workforce optimization, analyze the employee schedules, performance workloads and the other metrics and the outcomes are given the forecast details of the employee performance in next 30 days that are helped to get an idea about which is needed to be increased and improved.

  • Gathering the employee performance data and the employee work schedules by taking the attendance, performance data and other metrics.
  • Analyzing the data and ensuring that they are covered for all times.
  • Get the final output results and displayed the forecast of the upcoming monthly performance criteria and within that they can get an idea about which is needed to be increased.

Implement a route optimization for vehicles and forecasting upcoming delivery location Average count.

The research problem revolves around addressing the latency in implementing the best real-time route, improving integration with real-time update technologies, providing resources for real-time road condition and customer inputs, and resolving issues related to dynamic routing and delivery timing in the transportation and logistics industry.

According to the current records in surveys, there is no any implementation for raise vehicle timing for delivery completion and service coverage rate, for reducing cost and take good rating.Also previous route optimizations there's no gathering data from customer end and update the map real time disaster to show in user input path for destination. According to that need implement a route optimization for item delivery system.

  • Identify the traffic areas and take user updates for process easy way to destination.
  • Gathering and processing data in user inputs show the real time updates routes.
  • The processed data needs to be stored in a database for easily access to the route optimization function.
  • Analyzing data and forecasting the most packages delivered places for upcoming day.

Analyzing customer satisfaction regarding the item delivery through voice based feedback.

If the company is not actively analyzing and addressing customer feedback, it may miss opportunities to improve the delivery system and address any issues or concerns that customers may have. Some of the problems faced by customers in giving feedbacks are,Privacy and Security,Real Time Feedback,Feedback Quality,Timing,Low Response Rate,Implementation of feedback

To address the research problem of improving the delivery system by facilitating customer feedback, the solution involves implementing voice-based feedback with sentiment analysis. This approach enables real-time feedback collection, enhancing privacy and security measures. Sentiment analysis ensures feedback quality by gauging customer emotions, and timely feedback requests can boost response rates. Insights gained through sentiment analysis can drive effective implementation of feedback, enabling the company to proactively enhance the delivery system and resolve customer concerns.

  • Getting the voice based feedbacks from the customer with emotion through mobile app.
  • Passing data to trained model and Analyzing data and categorizing positive and Negative feedbacks.
  • Displaying a summary for all feedbacks for delivery rider and seller separately by mobile app.
